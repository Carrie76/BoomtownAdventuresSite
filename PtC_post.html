Pete the Cat Day

Pete the Cat Day returns for its fourth year, on August 6! It will be held at the Fort Payne Coal and Iron Building, and is presented by Fort Payne Main Street.

James Dean, author and creator of Pete the Cat and Fort Payne native, will be joining us to sign books and artwork!

Tickets are LIMITED! 100 tickets will be available at Katherine’s Gallery and Custom Framing July 19 – August 6, while they last. No purchase necessary. Katherine’s Gallery and Custom Framing will also be at the event with Pete the Cat books, t-shirts, and other items for sale.

Story times: 9:30, 10:30 & 11:30 at the Coal and Iron Building.
